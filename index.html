Interpretation of the graph

This graph uses a double Y-axis to compare changes in chemical dosing with changes in effluent quality over time.

• The right Y-axis represents Chemical Dose (mg/L), shown by the orange line. As the days progress, the chemical dose is steadily increased from Day 1 to Day 6.

• The left Y-axis represents Effluent TSS (mg/L), shown by the black line. Over the same time period, effluent TSS values decrease consistently.

• The opposite trends of the two lines indicate an inverse relationship: as chemical dose increases, effluent TSS decreases, suggesting improved solids removal performance.
